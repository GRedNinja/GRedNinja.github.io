Meet the Team

Gabriel Editor and Writer

Gabriel Moore is a native Texan who began his early adult life serving as a Hospital Corpsman in the United States Navy, caring for the sick and injured with steadiness and compassion. He later earned his undergraduate degree in computer science from Colorado Technical University and a master’s degree in data science from Regis University. Gabriel works as a software engineer in the aerospace domain and has also contributed to pediatric health care in both clinical and public school settings. Across these varied fields, he carries forward the Ignatian values of being a contemplative in action and seeking God in all things. He brings that same spirit to his work with Moore House Publishing, where he looks forward to building thoughtful, well‑crafted books that serve readers with clarity and purpose.
